I am a graduate student in the Taiwan International Graduate Program of Academia Sinica (and National Central University, Zhongli), Taiwan with a strong inclination towards data science, computational geophysics and web computing. For my PhD, I use seismic and GPS data to understand the crustal, mantle and core structure. Seismic data gives indirect capability to inspect the interior of our planet whereas GPS has incredible accuracy for understanding the crustal processes.

Many of my blog posts (and codes) are available on IESCODERS Blog.
